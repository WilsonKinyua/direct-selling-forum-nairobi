Direct Selling Summit & Expo

The Direct Selling Summit & Expo is a continental-wide stakeholder engagement platform that seeks to bring together key players within the Direct Selling industry, to explore the economic benefits of the Direct Selling Business on the African continent and the impact it can have on improving citizens' lives.

This yearâ€™s forum will be a two-day forum, which will take place in Nairobi Kenya, and will see the congregation of innovators within the industry, customers, decision-makers in the government and much more.

The forum will also provide a conducive environment for an expo, giving companies the opportunity to exhibit their products and services to the invited delegates.

Expected Outcomes

  • Learn how to successfully launch or relaunch your direct selling enterprise.
  • Master your online brand and combine personal selling with the most recent digital marketing tools.
  • Get clear, actionable advice on how to thrive in today's legal and regulatory environment.
  • Learn about emerging strategies, cutting-edge technologies, and best practices for taking your business to the next level.
  • Make contact with other direct selling company owners.
  • Learn what you should avoid doing that will harm your business.

The world is currently experiencing a global crisis that calls for innovative solutions that create a lasting impact on communities. We can best achieve this through social enterprises, as has been the case in the past, where such enterprises flourished in responding to global crises. The challenge is that a majority fail to build sustainable models that make them commercially viable, in the long run. It is therefore imperative to capitalise on the immense innovative energy of Africans and channel that towards social enterprise development.
